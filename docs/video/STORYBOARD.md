## Production Specifications

**Video Format:**
- Resolution: 1080x1920 (vertical for mobile) or 1920x1080 (horizontal for web)
- Frame rate: 30 fps
- Aspect ratio: 9:16 (mobile) or 16:9 (desktop)

**Color Palette:**
- Primary: XAI brand blue (#0066FF)
- Secondary: Electric purple (#7B61FF)
- Success: Green (#00D68F)
- Error: Red (#FF3366)
- Background: Dark theme (#1A1A1D)
- Text: White (#FFFFFF) / Light gray (#E5E5E5)

**Typography:**
- Headings: Inter Bold, 48pt
- Body: Inter Regular, 24pt
- Captions: Inter Medium, 18pt

**Animation Style:**
- Smooth transitions (0.3s ease-in-out)
- Finger tap: Scale + ripple effect
- Button press: 3D depth + shadow
- Success: Confetti particles + green glow
- Loading: Circular spinner (brand colors)

**Screen Recording:**
- Use iOS simulator or Android emulator for clean footage
- No status bar (clock, battery, signal)
- Focus mode: Highlight active UI elements
- Cursor: Custom finger pointer graphic

**Audio:**
- Background music: 80-100 BPM, upbeat electronic
- Sound effects: Button taps, success chimes, notification pings
- Voiceover: Clear, professional, friendly tone (male or female)
- Audio levels: Music 30%, SFX 50%, Voice 100%
